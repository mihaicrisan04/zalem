# 10 other bachelor thesis ideas for 2026

product-oriented computer science thesis ideas that are practical, modern, not too math-heavy, and include some useful LLM integration.

these are intentionally **not limited to e-commerce**.

---

## selection criteria

all ideas below are:

- practical to demo
- relevant in 2026
- strong for computer science
- product-oriented
- not dependent on heavy mathematics
- include LLMs in a meaningful but not necessarily dominant role

---

## 1. AI-assisted study planner for university students

### core idea

a system that helps students plan weekly study work, break down assignments, and detect risk of falling behind.

### why it is strong

- real problem
- easy to test with students
- clear product value
- mixes systems + UX + evaluation

### LLM role

- turn course deadlines into step-by-step plans
- rewrite tasks into clearer student-friendly language
- generate reflective check-in prompts

### CS angle

- scheduling logic
- task prioritization
- adaptive workflow design
- human-AI planning interface

### evaluation

- usefulness
- perceived organization
- task completion rate
- trust in suggestions

---
