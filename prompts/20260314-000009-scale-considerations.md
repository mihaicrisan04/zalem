> AI confirmed the concern was valid — the original recommendations plan targeted ~200 products and would break at 10K+. Created `docs/scale-considerations.md` identifying 7 things that break at scale: (1) content-based similarity needs vector search index instead of on-the-fly scoring, (2) co-occurrence needs batched pipeline instead of single action, (3) similar products needs precomputed top-N or vector search, (4) personalized "for you" needs two-stage candidate generation, (5) discount sort needs precomputed field + index, (6) seed data needs 5K-10K products from multiple sources, (7) schema needs embedding field, discountPercent field, and coOccurrenceAggregation table. Key principle: algorithms stay the same, but indexes and precomputation replace in-memory scanning.
